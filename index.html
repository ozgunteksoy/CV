Dr. Özgün Teksoy

I graduated in Pamukkale University at 2010. In undergraduate thesis, I studied the effects of Canola (rassica napus sp. Oleifera) extracts on rat hepatocytes under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Ramadan Mammadov. Also, I studied with sea turtles for two years in Kaska Lab.

During the master, I participated in several projects. In my master thesis, I researched to chlorogenic acid of radiosensitive effects on Caco2 (colorectal cancer cell line) cells in Gentotoxicity Lab. I got my master atgit Uludağ University on toxicology at 2013.

In 2019, I did a PhD in PhD at Eskişehir Osmangazi University on physiology. During my PhD, I studied with laboratory animals such as rat and mice. My PhD thesis is on investigating the protective effects of silymarin on thioacetamide-induced liver damage in Ayhanci Lab.
